Course Overview

Career Management (BNAD 596A) is designed to provide graduate students with the foundation to navigate their career search by providing knowledge, tools, and resources. This course applies career management theories and best practices to empower students to develop career readiness competencies in order to effectively manage their careers. In this course, students will work on developing a career management toolkit, building their professional development foundation, and allow for opportunities to gain knowledge through experiential learning.

Development of these competencies will happen through lecture, in-class activities, assignments, and projects including resume/cover letter development, interview preparation, informational interviewing, collaboration with peers, networking with Alumni, and engaging with guest speakers in relevant industries.

Course Goals, Objectives, & Learning Outcomes

Upon successful completion of this course, you should be able to:

  1. Use informational resources to explore career options & develop a career management toolkit
  2. Build a resume and cover letter as well as a professional network and connect with program and college alumni
  3. Apply the STAR method for behavioral based interviews and the TIARA framework for informational interviewing, and AI prompting for overall job search

Course Content

Developing A Career Management Toolkit

  • Identify priorities, preferences, and experiences to complete a job search in alignment with future career goals

Building Your Professional Development Foundation

  • Understand techniques to create an impactful resume and cover letter
  • Knowledge of ATS Systems & AI in relation to the job search
  • Understand the utilization of behavioral interview questions
  • Understand interview types & how to prepare for behavioral-based interviews utilizing the STAR Format
  • Learn the TIARA framework for Informational Interviewing

Experiential Learning

  • Apply teamwork, leadership, and technology skills by participating in networking events and Career Treks
  • Engage with industry and community leaders to expand your network, learn about career paths, and understand working in a corporate environment
